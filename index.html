About Me

As a visual artist, I like to work in different genres, using different styles and techniques: video art, video installation, video-poetry, experimental video, drawing-video-photo installation, video-performance, etc. I consider myself a video artist and I love creating with the camera. I like the movement in the video, the living breath. I'm trying to touch upon human equality, the role of women in society, the rights of humans, and the mentality of people of different cultural themes.
